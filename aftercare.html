Your piercing journey doesn't end when you leave our studio. Proper aftercare is essential for optimal healing, preventing infections, and ensuring your piercing looks beautiful for years to come. Follow our expert guidance for the best results.

Daily Cleaning

  • Clean hands thoroughly before touching your piercing
  • Use sterile saline solution or sea salt water (1/4 tsp per cup)
  • Gently clean 2-3 times daily
  • Pat dry with clean paper towel

What to Avoid

  • Don't touch or twist the jewelry unnecessarily
  • Avoid alcohol, hydrogen peroxide, or harsh chemicals
  • No swimming pools, hot tubs, or baths
  • Avoid makeup, lotions, or perfumes near the piercing

Healing Timeline

  • Earlobes: 6-8 weeks
  • Cartilage: 3-6 months
  • Nose: 2-4 months
  • Navel: 6-12 months

Warning Signs

  • Excessive redness, swelling, or heat
  • Yellow or green discharge
  • Severe pain or throbbing
  • Red streaking from the piercing site

Professional Tips for Optimal Healing

1

Sleep Smart: Use a travel pillow or sleep on the opposite side to avoid pressure on new piercings.

2

Jewelry Care: Don't change jewelry until fully healed. Only use high-quality materials like titanium or surgical steel.

3

Lifestyle: Maintain a healthy diet, stay hydrated, and get adequate sleep to support healing.

4

Follow-up: Contact us immediately if you notice any concerning symptoms or have questions.
